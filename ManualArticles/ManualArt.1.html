1. What is MiiTube and how to use it

-What is this?
MiiTube is a platform (created by Jacane_U) that collects all the videos and YouTube channels related to Miis, Wii, Wii U, 3DS, and more

-How to use it?
Using MiiTube is very simple. You can navigate through the different sections using the navigation bar at the top of the page.
-Videos Section: In the "Videos" section, you can browse and watch videos.
-MiiTubers Section: In the "MiiTubers" section, you can find a list of all the MiiTubers on the platform and their channels. You can click on a MiiTuber's name to view their profile and see all their videos.
-Manual Section: The "Manual" section provides helpful articles and guides on how to use MiiTube.
